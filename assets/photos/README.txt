Demonstration media — attribution & provenance
==============================================

This folder holds demonstration images used by the guqin technique reference.
Everything here is reproduced for a personal, non-commercial study page with
attribution. No copyright is claimed over the original works.

1. Finger-position photographs  (A1_*.jpg … A5_*.jpg)
   Source : peiyouqin.com  (NY Qin Society — Judy / Pei-You Chang)
   Pages  : https://peiyouqin.com/dazhi.html, shizhi.html, zhongzhi.html,
            mingzhi.html, gui.html   (graphics/*big.jpg)
   These show the basic left-hand finger-designation positions.

2. Poetic hand-gesture woodblock prints  (gestures/hand01.jpg … hand33.jpg)
   Source : silkqin.com — John Thompson
            https://www.silkqin.com/02qnpu/05tydq/ty3.htm
   Work   : Taiyin Daquanji (太音大全集), 1413 — the 33 shǒushì tú (手勢圖)
            poetic gesture-images, 17 right-hand + 16 left-hand.
   The woodblock prints are historical artworks; the photographic
   reproductions are John Thompson's. English image-titles in
   data/gestures.json paraphrase his translations — follow the source
   link above for the full verses and commentary.

Demonstration *videos* are NOT downloaded: the peiyouqin pages embed
YouTube clips, which the site plays inline (click-to-play, youtube-nocookie)
without redirecting.
